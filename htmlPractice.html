Low and Slow

This week I am extremely excited about a new cooking tec called sous vide. In souse vide cooking, you submerge the food (usually vacuum-sealed in plastic) into a water bath that is precisely set to the target teumperature you want to food to be cooked to. In his book, Cooking for Geeks, Jeff Potter desribes it as ultra-low temperature poaching
